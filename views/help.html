How to use Email Unsubscribe Manager

Sign in with Google, find and unsubscribe from marketing emails, then clean your inbox — all in one place.

Getting Started

After signing in with your Google account, you land on the main app with three tabs:

  • Emails — find emails with unsubscribe links and unsubscribe from senders.
  • Clean — move emails from senders you've already unsubscribed from to Gmail Trash in bulk.
  • Analytics — a bar chart showing how many marketing emails you've received per day over the past 7 or 30 days.

Click Fetch Emails in the Emails tab to scan your inbox for emails with unsubscribe links. Results are grouped by sender domain by default.

Choosing a Time Range

Use the time range buttons to control how far back to search:

  • Yesterday — emails from the past 24 hours. Fast and focused.
  • Last 7 Days — a week's worth of marketing email.
  • Last 30 Days — ideal for a thorough first-time clean-up.

After selecting a range, click Fetch Emails to load results.

Unsubscribing from Emails

Each row has an unsubscribe button that works in one of two ways:

  • Unsubscribe — opens the sender's unsubscribe page in a new tab. You may need to confirm on that page.
  • 1-Click Unsubscribe — for senders that support RFC 8058, the app sends the unsubscribe request on your behalf instantly. No page opens.
  • Unsubscribed — you've already unsubscribed from this sender. The row is dimmed as a reminder.

Every unsubscribe action is logged so the app can flag senders that email you again after you've unsubscribed.

Filtering, Sorting & Grouping

  • Search bar — type to instantly filter results by domain name or subject line.
  • Sort columns — click any column header (Domain, Sender, Subject, Date) to sort. Click again to reverse the order.
  • Group by Domain — collapses multiple emails from the same sender into one row. Click a group row to expand it.

Bulk Actions & Export

  • Select rows — check the checkbox on any row to select it. Check the header checkbox to select all visible rows.
  • Open Selected — opens the unsubscribe links for all selected rows at once in new tabs.
  • Export CSV — downloads the current (filtered) results as a spreadsheet. Appears once emails are loaded.

Clean Inbox

The Clean tab lets you bulk-remove emails from senders you've already unsubscribed from. It searches Gmail for emails from those senders and moves them to your Gmail Trash — they are recoverable from Trash for 30 days before Gmail permanently deletes them.

  • Only senders you've unsubscribed from inside this app are targeted — not your entire inbox.
  • Skip starred emails and Skip important emails options are checked by default to protect emails you've marked.
  • Up to 500 emails are processed per clean. If your inbox has more, a notice is shown — simply run Clean again.
  • A confirmation dialog appears before cleaning. You can suppress it via Settings → Inbox Clean → Skip confirmation.

Note: The Inbox Clean feature requires Gmail write access (to move emails to Trash). This permission is requested when you first sign in.

Settings

Click the gear icon in the top-right corner of the app to open the Settings panel. Your preferences are saved to your account and persist across devices.

  • Dark mode — toggle between light and dark themes. Can also be toggled from the moon/sun icon in the header.
  • Skip confirmation — suppress the confirmation dialog that appears before each Inbox Clean.
  • Delete Account — permanently removes your profile, all unsubscribe history, analytics data, and active sessions from the app. Type DELETE to confirm. This cannot be undone.

Admin Panel

Admin only

The Admin Panel is accessible from the Admin link in the top-right corner of the app (visible to admins only). It has three tabs:

Users

Manage who can access the app. Only users on the allowlist can sign in.

  • Add user — enter a Gmail address and optionally grant admin rights. The user can sign in immediately after being added.
  • Enable / Disable — toggle a user's active status. Disabled users cannot sign in and existing sessions are invalidated.
  • Toggle Admin — grant or revoke admin privileges for any user.
  • Remove — permanently removes a user from the allowlist. Their session is terminated immediately.

Active Sessions

View all currently signed-in users, including their IP address, browser, and when their session expires. Use Terminate to force-sign-out any session immediately — useful if an account may be compromised.

Unsubscribe History

An audit log of every unsubscribe action taken across all users — who unsubscribed, from which domain, the sender address, how many times, and when. Useful for spotting senders that keep emailing after being unsubscribed.

Privacy & Permissions

  • The app requests Gmail access to read your inbox and move emails to Trash (required for Inbox Clean). It can never send emails or permanently delete them — only move them to Trash, where they remain recoverable for 30 days.
  • Email content is never stored — only unsubscribe actions (domain, sender, date) are saved to the database.
  • Sessions expire automatically after 24 hours (or 30 days if "Remember me" was selected at sign-in).
  • Access is restricted to email addresses on the allowlist — only admins can grant access to new users.
